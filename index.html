About

I am a Mechanical Engineering graduate from the University of Waterloo in the class of 2020. I currently work as an E.I.T at IBI group, where I design mechanical systems for buildings.

One of my ongoing projects is the Broadway Subway Project in Vancouver. I am involved in the mechanical design for the Mount Pleasant and Oak Stations. This involves design of the HVAC, drainage, plumbing and fire protection systems. Due to the stations being underground, this project poses some very interesting challenges in design and construction.

Another project that I am currently working on is Toronto Accessibility Upgrades for the City of Toronto. This project involves upgrading existing City of Toronto buildings to be accessible and compliant the latest accessibility standards. This is a multi-year project involving hundreds of buildings, which has given me a chance to gain experience on a variety of building types and use-cases. Some of the buildings I have worked on so far include Metro Hall and North York Civic Center.

I also recently finished my internship as a Product Analyst at Droit Financial Technologies LLC in New York, where I spent a lot of time reading financial regulations for work and transcribing them into software can then be used to automate transaction reporting, follow transparency requirements, etc.
