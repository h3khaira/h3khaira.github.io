Stewart Platform

A Stewart Platform is a 6 degree of freedom robotic device that is able to manipulate its platform in a highly flexible and robust manner. As a part of a 3rd year design project, my group and I designed a stewart platform that was able to guide a ball through a maze.

Airfoil Panel Method

A python implementation for the vortex panel method that outputs a plot of Lift Coefficient (Cl) vs Angle of Attack. This implementation works for any NACA 4-digit airfoil. This can be found on my Github.
